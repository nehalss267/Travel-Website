Most famous places

Recommended

New York City

New York City offers iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Times Square, world-class museums such as the MET, and a vibrant, bustling city life. Explore Central Park, walk across the Brooklyn Bridge, or catch a Broadway show!

London

London is a city rich in history, with landmarks like Big Ben, the Tower of London, and Buckingham Palace. Take a scenic walk along the Thames, visit world-famous museums like the British Museum, or enjoy a West End show.

Paris

The city of love, Paris is known for its beautiful architecture, including the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame. Stroll along the Seine River, explore the Louvre Museum, and indulge in world-class cuisine and shopping on the Champs-Élysées.

Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is home to ancient ruins like the Colosseum and Roman Forum. Wander through the streets of Vatican City, visit the Sistine Chapel, and immerse yourself in the art, history, and culture of Italy's capital.

Tokyo

Tokyo is a futuristic metropolis where traditional temples meet cutting-edge technology. Experience the vibrant city life, visit the serene Meiji Shrine, or take a walk through the iconic Shibuya Crossing and try authentic Japanese cuisine.

Dubai

Dubai is famous for its ultramodern architecture, including the Burj Khalifa, luxury shopping at the Dubai Mall, and stunning desert landscapes. Explore the city’s opulent hotels, or enjoy a desert safari for an unforgettable experience.
